About PPP ///

People+Planet Project

The People + Planet Project creates campaigns to coincide with UN Sustainable Development Goal Announcements and COP Climate Conferences. Our mission is to distill these global events into approachable calls—and means—to individual action. Whether it be an issue of poverty, hunger, equality, clean water, renewable energy or climate change, the content and tools we create are all designed with the people and the planet first in mind and first at heart.

These commitments taken together should lay the foundation for addressing the world’s most pressing challenges. This is the year we need to take individual empowered action, connect collectively, and demand real action and accountability from our world leaders.

We stand at the turning point for the future of people and the planet. We must accelerate action and leave no individual or country behind. What happens in 2015 will make an enormous impact on our lives and our children’s lives. Today is the moment. It is the shifting of the tides. And the People + Planet Project will give you the real tools to make the shift meaningful.

The first large-scale campaign out of the People + Planet Project is Every Dollar Is a Vote. We believe that every dollar you spend has an impact on the planet and it’s people. We buy products every day just to survive. Why not buy these products from companies and manufactures that will help push the world forward, and not destroy it.

The companies we buy from should be held to the highest environmental, anti-slavery, pro-equality, and pro-women standards to benefit the greater good, that’s where we should be investing our money to make our world a better, more sustainable place to live.

Through this campaign we will build the necessary tools to engage a broader audience in three main categories: ecommerce shopping, investing and divesting. For the first time we will empower the world to vote for the world we want to see. This campaign is more than a talking point, a petition or a hashtag. It’s a powerful set of tools that turns something you already do into a catalyst for positive change in the world.
